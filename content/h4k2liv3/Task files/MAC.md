- stands for Media Access Control. its kind of like a fingerprint in a way because its basically a unique hardware identifier that uniquely identifies each [[NIC]] (network interface card) on a local network. its permanently burned into the hardware when its made
- it operates in the datalink layer of the [[OSI model]] 
- written as 6 groups of two hexadecimal digits, separated by colons/hyphens
	- example: 00:1A:2B:3C:4D:5E
