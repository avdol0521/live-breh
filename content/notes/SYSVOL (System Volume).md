---
title: "SYSVOL (System Volume)"
tags:
  - fetus
---
- SYSVOL is a shared folder on every DC that stores logon scripts, [[Group Policy Objects (GPO)]] and other domain wide files that need to be synced across all DCs, also known as [[SYSVOL Replication]]
- stored at the location `C:\Windows\SYSVOL\sysvol`
