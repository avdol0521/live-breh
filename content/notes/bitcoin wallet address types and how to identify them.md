## wallet formats:
#### P2PKH (Pay to Public Key Hash(Legacy)):
- stars with `1`
#### P2SH (Pay to Script Hash):
- starts with `3`
#### SegWit/Bech32:
- starts with `bc1`
## wallet transaction viewing:
- use https://www.blockchain.com/explorer and just search the wallet address
